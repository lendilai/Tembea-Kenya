Tembea Africa

Kenya is endowed with world-class resorts that spread out across the whole country. However, The Ark Country Resort in Nyeri is one in a million. The Ark is located within the Aberdares and it name comes as a result of its resemblance to Noah's Ark in the bible. The journey to The Ark from Nairobi is a whooping four hour drive. You will drive to Mweiga; which is past Nyeri on your way to Nyahururu, and then you will divert from the main road so as to get to your destination. The weather there is quite chilly thus you will have to carry a warm pullover with you. The final stretch is also on a murram truck so remember to lock your windows.
